Murrah buffalo is a well-known dairy buffalo breed originating from Haryana and Punjab in India.

Characteristics:
Murrah buffaloes have a shiny black coat and tightly curled horns.

Milk Production:
They produce about 1800–3000 kg of milk per lactation with high fat content.

Adaptability:
Murrah buffaloes adapt well to different climatic conditions.

Uses:
They are mainly used for milk production.

Maintenance:
Murrah buffaloes require nutritious feed including green fodder and concentrate mixtures. Buffaloes also need access to water for drinking and wallowing. Proper shelter and veterinary care are necessary.
