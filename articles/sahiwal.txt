Sahiwal cattle is a well-known dairy breed originating from the Punjab region.

Characteristics:
They have a reddish-brown coat and loose skin.

Milk Production:
Sahiwal cows produce around 2000–3000 kg of milk per lactation.

Adaptability:
They are tolerant to heat and resistant to many diseases.

Uses:
Mainly used for dairy production.

Maintenance:
They require balanced feeding, proper shelter, and regular veterinary care. Adequate water supply and clean housing conditions help maintain their productivity.
