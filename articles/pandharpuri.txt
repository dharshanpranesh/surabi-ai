Pandharpuri buffalo is a breed from Maharashtra, India.

Characteristics:
They are known for their long curved horns.

Milk Production:
They produce around 1500–2000 kg of milk per lactation.

Adaptability:
They are well adapted to hot and dry conditions.

Uses:
They are mainly used for milk production.

Maintenance:
They require balanced feeding, clean drinking water, and proper shelter. Regular health checkups help maintain their productivity.
