Ayrshire cattle originated from Scotland.

Characteristics:
They have a reddish-brown and white coat.

Milk Production:
Ayrshire cows produce about 5000–7000 kg of milk per lactation.

Adaptability:
They adapt well to different environments.

Uses:
They are mainly raised for dairy farming.

Maintenance:
They require good quality feed, clean water, and proper shelter. Regular health checkups and vaccination are important for maintaining productivity.
