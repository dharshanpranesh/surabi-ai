Jaffrabadi buffalo is a breed originating from Gujarat, India.

Characteristics:
They are large buffaloes with heavy heads and drooping horns.

Milk Production:
Milk production ranges from 1000–2500 kg per lactation.

Adaptability:
They are well suited to hot climates.

Uses:
Mainly used for dairy production.

Maintenance:
They require green fodder, dry fodder, and concentrate feed. Clean drinking water and regular veterinary care are important for maintaining their health.
