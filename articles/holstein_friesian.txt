Holstein Friesian cattle originated in the Netherlands and Germany.

Characteristics:
They are large animals with distinctive black and white markings.

Milk Production:
Holstein cows are famous for high milk production, usually producing 6000–10000 kg of milk per lactation.

Adaptability:
They perform well in moderate climates but can adapt to various environments with proper care.

Uses:
They are widely used for commercial dairy farming.

Maintenance:
Holstein cattle require high-quality feed, including green fodder, silage, and concentrate mixtures. Clean housing and proper milking management are necessary. Regular veterinary care and vaccinations are important.
