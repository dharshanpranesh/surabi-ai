Brown Swiss cattle originated from Switzerland.

Characteristics:
They have a brown coat and strong body structure.

Milk Production:
Brown Swiss cows produce around 6000–8000 kg of milk per lactation.

Adaptability:
They are known for their strength and adaptability.

Uses:
They are primarily used for milk production.

Maintenance:
They require balanced nutrition, proper housing, and routine veterinary care. Clean water and hygienic milking practices are essential.
